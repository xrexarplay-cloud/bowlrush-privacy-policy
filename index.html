BowlRush – Privacy Policy

Developed by XR Horizon Pvt. Ltd.
Effective Date: 30 September 2025

1. Introduction

XR Horizon Pvt. Ltd. is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BowlRush handles your information while you play our single-player bowling VR game on Meta Quest devices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone, or address)
  • No sensitive information (financial, biometric, or health data)
  • No ads, third-party trackers, or analytics

3. Information Stored Locally

The game stores only limited data on your device:

  • Gameplay progress (scores, achievements, levels)
  • Player preferences (controls, audio settings)

This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the game.

4. Data Security

All game-related data is stored locally on your device. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the risk of external data breaches is eliminated.

5. Children’s Privacy

BowlRush is intended for players aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or guardians may contact us with any concerns.

6. Third-Party Services

No third-party SDKs, cloud services, or integrations are used that collect or process your data.

7. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, you are not required to request access, correction, or deletion. You may:

  • Uninstall the game to remove all stored data
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted here with the updated effective date.
